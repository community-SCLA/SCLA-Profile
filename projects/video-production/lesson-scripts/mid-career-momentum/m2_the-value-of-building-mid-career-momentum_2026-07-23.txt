*You’ve accumulated years of experience—projects delivered, teams supported, stakeholders managed. But your next step probably feels less clear than your first job did*

*In this module, we zoom out and simplify your options into three common paths: promotion in your current lane, a lateral move to a better-fit role, or redesigning your current role to better match how you work and what you care about.*

*A promotion path focuses on deepening your influence and scope where you already are. A lateral move centers on moving to a new department, organization, or function that better fits your strengths. Role redesign is about reshaping your responsibilities, focus, or schedule without leaving entirely—often by negotiating new projects, boundaries, or hybrid responsibilities.*

*None of these paths is “better” in general. The right path is the one that aligns with your values, energizes you, and fits your life constraints right now. *

You have years of experience behind you. Projects delivered. Teams supported. Stakeholders managed. And still, your next step probably feels less clear than your first job did.

In this module, we zoom out together and simplify your options into three common paths.

The first is a promotion in your current lane. That path focuses on deepening your influence and your scope right where you already are.

The second is a lateral move to a better-fit role. That means moving to a new department, a new organization, or a new function that fits your strengths more closely.

The third is redesigning the role you are already in, so it matches how you work and what you care about. That means reshaping your responsibilities, your focus, or your schedule without leaving entirely, often by negotiating new projects, new boundaries, or hybrid responsibilities.

Promotion. Lateral move. Role redesign. Which one sounds like your next move?

None of these paths is better in general. The right path is the one that aligns with your values, energizes you, and fits the life constraints you are living with right now.

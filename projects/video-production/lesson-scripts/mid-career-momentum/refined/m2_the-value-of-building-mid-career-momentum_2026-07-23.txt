You've accumulated years of experience by now. Projects delivered, teams supported, stakeholders managed. But your next step probably feels less clear than your first job did.

So in this module, we zoom out and simplify your options into three common paths: a promotion in your current lane, a lateral move to a better-fit role, or a redesign of the role you're already in to better match how you work and what you care about.

A promotion path is about deepening your influence and scope right where you are. A lateral move is about crossing into a new department, organization, or function that fits your strengths better. And a role redesign is about reshaping your responsibilities, your focus, or your schedule without leaving entirely — often by negotiating new projects, new boundaries, or hybrid responsibilities.

Here's the honest part: none of these paths is "better" in general. The right one is the path that lines up with your values, energizes you, and fits the life constraints you're actually living with right now.

Lesson: Mod 5 — Momentum Snapshot
Title: You've Built Momentum

Module framing: You've defined your next move, repositioned your experience, and built a strategic outreach list. This final module ties it all together. You'll create your Momentum Snapshot — a single page that names one outcome and three actions you'll take to move toward it — and close the track with a reflection and a next step.

[Open — instructor on camera]

If you're watching this, you've finished Mid-Career Momentum. That's worth pausing on for a moment.

When people start this track, they usually arrive with one of two feelings — a quiet frustration that things haven't moved the way they'd hoped, or a growing ambition that hasn't found its focus yet. Either way, most people start with a lot of noise in their head and very little on paper.

Look at what you've done since then.

[Cut to graphic — track summary appears on screen]

In Module 1, you defined your next move — a specific target, not a vague wish.

In Module 2, you repositioned your experience so your resume, headline, and summary all point in the same direction.

In Module 3, you mapped the peers, sponsors, and opportunity holders who matter most for that move — and you sent at least one real message.

In Module 4, you gauged your confidence with AI and started shaping how you'll adapt as your field keeps shifting.

And just now, in Module 5, you translated all of that into a Momentum Snapshot — one outcome and three actions you can actually finish.

[Return to instructor]

Here's what I want you to notice. You didn't just take a course. You built a system. You now have language for your next move, materials that match that direction, a real network you're activating, and a written plan for the next few weeks. That's a different starting position than the one you had when you began.

[Cut to graphic — three reminders slide up one at a time]

Three things to remember as you leave this track.

One — momentum decays if you don't touch it. Put a recurring reminder on your calendar — thirty days from today — to reopen your Momentum Snapshot. Check what you finished. Update what you didn't. Replace what you completed with the next action.

Two — the framework is reusable. Outcome, visibility, relationships, results. When your goal changes — and it will — the four categories still apply. You've learned a way of thinking, not just a plan.

Three — you are not doing this alone. The Career Accelerator has more tracks, more tools, and a member community. Every conversation, every session, every next track adds to the same body of work.

[Return to instructor, warmer close]

Whatever your next move ends up being — the promotion, the redesigned role, the lateral move that finally fits — it will be built the same way this track was built. One action at a time. Small. Consistent. Compounding.

You've earned this completion. Now let's talk about what's next.

[End card — "Complete Your Reflection & Choose Your Next Step"]

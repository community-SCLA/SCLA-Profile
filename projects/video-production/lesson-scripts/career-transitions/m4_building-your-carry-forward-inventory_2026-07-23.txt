Header: What Deserves to Come With You

The Carry-Forward Inventory is a structured list of what you built in your prior career that is genuinely portable into your next chapter. Not everything is. Some things belong to the old context. But the honest inventory is longer than most career changers realize — and building it explicitly is one of the single highest-leverage exercises in this whole track.

P.S. - This is important
If you need to ask a peer, mentor, colleague, or collaborator for a testimonial of your work and
character, we recommend leveraging LinkedIn’s “request a recommendation” tool. It makes the
process quick and easy, organizing your recommendations in a centralized location while adding
more value to your profile. More about LinkedIn in the next module.
Here’s our typical order of operations:
● DM them with the request that LinkedIn will auto-generate for you
● Send them a personal email or text (depending on your dynamics) saying something like
“Hey! How’s your day going? I’m in the process of updating my professional assets, and
I’d be so grateful if you could provide a testimonial that I can share when pitching myself
for new opportunities. I sent you a LinkedIn request for ease, but let me know if it would
be better to circle back with you on this another time. Thank you SO much for your
continued support!”

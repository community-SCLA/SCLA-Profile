# SCLA MEMBERSHIP TEAM TWO-TIER COMMUNICATION SOP

Responses

MEMBER COMMUNICATION SOP

Department: 
Applies To: All inbound email, help desk, and portal inquiries
Response SLA: 24 business hours

🔹 TIER 1: NON-MEMBERS

(Prospects | Invitation Only | General Interest | Pre-Payment)

1. ABOUT SCLA INQUIRY

Use When:

Asks what SCLA is

Parent or advisor requests an explanation

Questions legitimacy

General information inquiry

Action Steps (Internal):

Confirm the individual is not yet a paid member

Respond using the approved overview template

Offer to answer additional questions

Email Template:

Subject: About SCLA Membership

Hi [First Name],

The Society for Collegiate Leadership & Achievement (SCLA) is an accredited honor society that recognizes high-achieving college students and provides career-ready tools to support success during college and beyond.

Membership includes access to professional development programs, career readiness certifications, networking opportunities, scholarships, and leadership resources designed to help you stand out academically and professionally.

Please let us know if you have any questions about membership or next steps.

Best regards,
SCLA Membership Team

2. MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS INQUIRY

Use When:

Aasks what is included

Compared to other honor societies

Asking about value

Action Steps:

Reinforce career-focused value

Maintain a confident, non-defensive tone

Encourage follow-up

Email Template:

Subject: SCLA Membership Benefits

Hi [First Name],

SCLA membership goes beyond recognition. Members receive access to:

• Career Readiness Certifications focused on real-world skills
• Opportunities to earn college credit at no additional cost through select programs
• AI-powered networking with peers, mentors, and professionals
• Exclusive scholarships, internships, and job opportunities
• Leadership development resources and digital credentials

You can explore all the benefits in more detail on our website, and we’re happy to answer any questions you may have.

Best regards,
SCLA Membership Team

3. MEMBERSHIP FEE QUESTIONS

Use When:

Asks about cost

asks about recurring dues

Action Steps:

Clearly state a one-time lifetime fee

Mention no annual dues

Offer payment options if needed

Email Template:

Subject: SCLA Membership Fee

Hi [First Name],

Thank you for reaching out! SCLA has a one-time lifetime membership fee of $95. There are no annual dues, and this fee provides full access to all programs, certifications, resources, and exclusive member opportunities.

If cost is a concern, we do offer flexible payment options to help make membership more accessible.

Please let us know if you have any other questions!

Best regards,
SCLA Membership Team

says they cannot afford it right now

SCLA Membership Team

COURSE DESCRIPTION

SCLA Career Readiness Certification – ISPI™️ is a comprehensive certification course that integrates career development, leadership training, and strengths-based assessment using the Innovation Strengths Preferences Indicator™️ (ISPI™️). This course helps students understand how their natural preferences influence communication, decision-making, problem solving, and career choices.

Students complete the ISPI™️ assessment and use the results to guide personal development, career exploration, and leadership growth. The course combines self-assessment, reflection, goal setting, and applied exercises to help students develop confidence, direction, and professional readiness.

Throughout the course, students explore career interests, develop productive habits, improve communication skills, and learn how to apply their strengths in academic, workplace, and leadership settings. The course emphasizes self-awareness, adaptability, and intentional growth as essential skills for success in the modern world.

By the end of the course, students will create a personal career readiness plan based on their strengths profile and future goals.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Complete the ISPI™️ assessment
• Identify personal strengths and preferences
• Understand different working styles
• Improve communication awareness
• Apply strengths to career planning
• Develop professional habits
• Practice leadership skills
• Set academic and career goals
• Reflect on personal growth
• Create a career readiness plan

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Students must complete all assignments with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will present a Career Readiness Plan based on ISPI™️ results, including strengths, career goals, communication style, and personal development strategy.

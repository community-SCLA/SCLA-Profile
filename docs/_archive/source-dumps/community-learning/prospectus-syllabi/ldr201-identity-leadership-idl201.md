COURSE DESCRIPTION

Identity Leadership is a foundational leadership development course focused on understanding how personal identity influences behavior, decision-making, communication, and leadership effectiveness. The course introduces students to the idea that strong leadership begins with self-awareness and the ability to understand one’s values, strengths, beliefs, and motivations.

Students will explore how identity shapes the way people think, act, and respond to challenges. Through guided reflection, assessments, and leadership exercises, students will learn how to recognize their natural tendencies and develop the ability to lead with clarity and purpose. The course also emphasizes responsibility, consistency, and personal growth as essential elements of leadership.

The concept of identity leadership teaches that individuals become stronger leaders when they understand who they are and intentionally align their actions with their goals and values. Students will practice applying this awareness in academic, professional, and team situations.

By the end of the course, students will have a clear understanding of their leadership identity and a framework for continued personal and professional development.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand the concept of identity in leadership
• Explore personal values and beliefs
• Identify strengths and growth areas
• Develop self-awareness
• Understand how identity affects behavior
• Improve confidence and responsibility
• Learn leadership fundamentals
• Align actions with goals
• Build consistency and discipline
• Create a personal leadership plan

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Assignments must be completed with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Students will present a Personal Leadership Plan describing their identity, values, strengths, goals, and the actions they will take to grow as a leader.

# CAR105: Social Media University

GENERAL INFORMATION

Email: awestby@thescla.org

COURSE DESCRIPTION

Social Media University is a career readiness course designed to teach students how to use social media professionally, responsibly, and strategically. In today’s world, online presence plays a major role in career opportunities, networking, entrepreneurship, and leadership. This course helps students understand how to build a positive digital identity and use social platforms to support personal and professional goals.

Students will learn how to create a professional profile, develop a personal brand, communicate effectively online, and avoid common mistakes that damage reputation. The course also explores how social media can be used for networking, job searching, content creation, and business development.

Throughout the course, students will complete practical exercises, reflections, and projects that help them build real skills they can use immediately. By the end of the course, students will have a clear social media strategy and a professional online presence aligned with their career goals.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand the role of social media in career development
• Build a professional online profile
• Develop a personal brand
• Learn responsible digital behavior
• Practice professional communication online
• Create useful content
• Use social media for networking
• Learn job search strategies using social platforms
• Protect digital reputation
• Create a social media career plan

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Students must complete assignments with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will present a Professional Social Media Strategy including personal brand, profile updates, networking plan, and career goals.

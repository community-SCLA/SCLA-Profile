# LDR102: Brand You — Building Your Unique Professional Identity for Amplified Impact

COURSE DESCRIPTION

Brand You: Building Your Unique Professional Identity for Amplified Impact is a career development course focused on helping students define, develop, and communicate a strong professional identity. In today’s competitive and AI-driven workplace, success depends not only on skills, but also on how clearly you present your value, strengths, and purpose to others.

This course guides students through the process of discovering their unique strengths, clarifying their professional message, and learning how to consistently present themselves with confidence and authenticity. Students will learn how to build a personal brand that reflects their values, communicates their abilities, and increases their visibility in academic, professional, and leadership environments.

Through reflection exercises, branding activities, storytelling practice, and real-world applications, students will develop a clear professional identity and learn how to use it to create opportunities, build trust, and increase influence.

By the end of the course, students will have a complete personal branding system they can use throughout their career.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Define their personal strengths and values
• Identify their unique professional qualities
• Develop a clear personal brand message
• Improve confidence in self-presentation
• Practice professional storytelling
• Learn how to communicate value to employers
• Build a consistent professional image
• Strengthen leadership presence
• Use branding in networking and interviews
• Create a personal brand portfolio

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Assignments must be completed with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will present a complete personal branding portfolio including their professional story, strengths, values, and strategy for using their brand to create career opportunities.

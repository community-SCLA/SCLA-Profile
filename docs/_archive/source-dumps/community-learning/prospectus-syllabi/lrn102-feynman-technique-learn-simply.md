COURSE DESCRIPTION

Feynman Technique: Learn Simply is a learning skills course designed to help students understand complex ideas by explaining them in clear, simple language. The Feynman Technique is a proven method used by scientists, educators, and professionals to improve understanding, memory, and problem solving.

This course teaches students how to break down difficult concepts, identify gaps in knowledge, and rebuild understanding step by step. Instead of memorizing information, students will practice explaining ideas as if teaching someone else, which leads to deeper comprehension and long-term retention.

Students will apply the Feynman Technique to academic subjects, professional topics, and real-world skills. The course also develops confidence in speaking, writing, and presenting ideas clearly.

By the end of the course, students will be able to learn faster, study more effectively, and communicate knowledge in a way that others can easily understand.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand the Feynman learning method
• Break down complex ideas
• Identify gaps in understanding
• Practice explaining concepts simply
• Improve memory and retention
• Strengthen study skills
• Build confidence in communication
• Learn active learning strategies
• Apply the method to different subjects
• Develop a personal learning system

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Assignments must be completed with a score of 80% or higher.

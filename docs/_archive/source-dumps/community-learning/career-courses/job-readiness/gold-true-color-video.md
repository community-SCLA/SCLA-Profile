# Gold True Color Video  

**Slide 1**

Gold :  True Colors

---

**Slide 2**

GOLD
Strengths:
Reliable and responsible
Excellent at organizing and managing tasks
Values punctuality and deadlines
Follows through on commitments
Challenges :
May resist change or dislike ambiguity
Can be overly critical of themselves or others
Sometimes struggles with flexibility
Examples:
A student who always has their homework done on time
A team captain who keeps everyone on schedule
An accountant or project manager who ensures everything runs smoothly

---

**Slide 3**

Using  GOLD  to Your Advantage
In School:  Take on leadership roles in clubs or group projects where organization is key. Help classmates stay on track.
In Careers:  Consider fields like management, education, or administration where your reliability is valued.
Personal Growth:  Work on flexibility and adapting to change when things don’t go as planned.
Remember:  No color is better or worse than another. Each brings unique strengths to a group or workplace. By understanding your True Colors, you can play to your strengths, work on your challenges, and appreciate the diversity in others—setting yourself up for success in school, work, and life!

This document covers two things: (1) the simple step-by-step plan we can run this semester using email and Zoom, and (2) the 12 questions the team needs to answer before we can start. Bring this to our next team meeting.

What We Can Run Right Now

The entire member journey runs on three things we already have: email, Zoom, and a shared spreadsheet. Here is what that looks like in practice.

What to say out loud while the slide is up

5 things every pledge should walk away knowing

1.   Within 24 hours you get an email inviting you to a live Zoom — do not ignore it

2.   On that Zoom you answer 8 quick questions about your goals and career

3.   We place you in a track: Accelerator, Momentum, or Reinvention

4.   You get a personal email: your track, your first 3 modules, and your first meeting date

5.   You choose how to engage: solo, small group, or hybrid — no wrong choice

•     One ask before the call: log in and start your portfolio

•     Zoom RSVP link or calendar invite

Run of show — minute by minute

6.   0:00–0:05  Welcome. Ask members to drop school + degree in chat. Read schools aloud — creates instant community.

7.   0:05–0:12  SCLA in 5 minutes. One slide. Three things only: certification, CEO series, mentor network.

8.   0:12–0:20  Launch the Zoom poll. 8 questions, 5 minutes. Read results aloud after each question. Co-host logs tracks.

9.   0:20–0:28  Track reveal. Announce the breakdown. One sentence per track. Ask 1–2 people per track to say hello.

10.0:28–0:42  Dashboard tour — three things only: where modules live, the events calendar, and how to find and start the portfolio.

11.0:42–0:55  Q&A. Co-host drops 3 links in chat: dashboard login, Journey Meeting dates per track, any campus peer workaround.

12.0:55–1:00  One ask only: complete your first module before Friday. Under 30 minutes. That is the only job this week.

What to show on the dashboard tour — with the current dashboard

•     Modules: Walk them to the course library. Tell them their track email tonight will give them the exact 3 module names to search for. Do not let them browse now.

•     Events calendar: Show where it lives and how to RSVP to Journey Meetings and hosted events.

•     Portfolio: This is the most important. Show where it is and tell them to start filling it out before doing anything else. This is the key to a successful dashboard journey.

Three engagement options — member chooses in the placement email

•     Solo: work through modules at own pace, no check-ins

•     Small group: matched with 2–3 others on same track, one check-in per week by email or group chat

•     Hybrid: solo modules but attends the monthly Journey Meeting — no weekly check-ins

14-day nudge email — send manually if no engagement

Who does this

Coach runs each track separately — three 45-minute Zooms, same week, different groups. Three separate Zoom links.

Agenda — 45 minutes

17.Welcome round — name, school, one word about their first month  (5 min)

18.Module debrief — what did you complete? What landed?  (10 min)

19.Open floor — wins, questions, coach facilitates  (10 min)

20.Phase 2 preview — coach announces next 3 modules per track via email after the call  (10 min)

21.Accountability pairing — members pair up for the next 30 days  (5 min)

22.Close — one module to complete before the next meeting  (5 min)

Monthly rhythm

•     Journey Meeting per track — three track-specific Zooms, coach-led, 45 min each

•     One general module sprint Zoom — open to all tracks, all work through the same module together

•     Hosted calendar events tied to track themes — email each track which events are most relevant for them

•     14-day nudge email to any member who hasn't engaged

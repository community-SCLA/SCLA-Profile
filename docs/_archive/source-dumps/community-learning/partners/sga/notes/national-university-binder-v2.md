# National University - Binder (v2)

SCLA Invitation Summary & Cycle Confirmation
Thank you for your coordination with the SCLA team as we prepare to launch the Fall 2025
invitation cycle for eligible students at National University. Below is a summary of what
students will receive and the timing of each touchpoint.
Invitation Cycle Overview – Summer 2025
• July 30, 2025 – Email Announcement
• August 2, 2025 – Paper Invitation Mailed
• August 12, 2025 – Postcard Reminder Mailed
• September 2, 2025 – Email: Deadline Reminder
• September 3, 2025 – Email: Final Day to Accept
• September 5, 2025 – Email: Deadline Extension
• September 8, 2025 – SCLA Orientation for National University students
Students who begin but do not complete their membership registration will also receive
follow-up messages encouraging them to finalize enrollment before orientation.
Standard Invitation Format
All invitations follow a national format that is personalized for each participating campus.
For National University, the materials reflect elements including the advisor’s name and
signature, a brief statement of support, orientation date, university name and logo, and—
when applicable—a Chapter President’s signature.
This consistent approach ensures a professional, locally aligned invitation experience for
your students.
Through a special partnership with Stedman Graham — renowned leadership expert and
creator of Identity Leadership™ — qualifying National University students are offered
exclusive access to add this self-leadership program to strengthen their SCLA experience.

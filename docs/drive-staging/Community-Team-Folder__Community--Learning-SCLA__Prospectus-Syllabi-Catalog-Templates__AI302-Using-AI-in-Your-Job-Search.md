# AI302: Using AI in Your Job Search

COURSE DESCRIPTION

Using AI in Your Job Search is a career development course designed to help students use artificial intelligence tools to improve every stage of the job search process. As hiring practices continue to evolve, job seekers who understand how to use AI effectively can save time, produce stronger applications, and make better career decisions.

This course teaches students how to use AI to research companies, generate ideas, improve résumés, write cover letters, prepare for interviews, and organize their job search strategy. Students will also learn how to evaluate AI responses, avoid common mistakes, and use technology responsibly while maintaining authenticity and professionalism.

Through guided exercises and real-world activities, students will practice using AI tools to complete job search tasks more efficiently. By the end of the course, students will have a complete, AI-supported job search system they can use to find opportunities, prepare applications, and increase their chances of getting interviews and job offers.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand how AI can support job searching
• Use AI to research careers and companies
• Improve résumé and cover letter writing
• Use AI for networking messages
• Prepare for interviews using AI tools
• Organize job search activities
• Evaluate AI-generated content
• Avoid over-reliance on automation
• Build a structured job search system
• Create an AI-supported job search plan

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Students must complete assignments with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will create a complete AI-Supported Job Search System including résumé improvements, company research, outreach messages, interview preparation, and a structured application plan.

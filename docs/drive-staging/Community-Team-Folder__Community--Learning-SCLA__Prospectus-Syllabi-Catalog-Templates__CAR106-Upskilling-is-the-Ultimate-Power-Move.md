# CAR106: Upskilling is the Ultimate Power Move

COURSE DESCRIPTION

Upskilling is the Ultimate Power Move is a career development course designed to help students understand the importance of continuous learning in a rapidly changing world. As technology, automation, and artificial intelligence reshape the workplace, the ability to learn new skills quickly has become one of the most valuable professional advantages.

This course teaches students how to identify the skills they need, choose the right learning opportunities, and build a personal strategy for long-term growth. Students will explore how employers evaluate talent, why adaptability matters, and how upskilling can lead to better opportunities, higher income potential, and stronger career security.

Through reflection, planning exercises, and applied activities, students will develop a personal upskilling roadmap that helps them stay competitive, confident, and prepared for future challenges.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand the importance of lifelong learning
• Identify current and future skill needs
• Evaluate strengths and gaps
• Learn how to choose the right skills to develop
• Build a personal upskilling plan
• Improve learning efficiency
• Develop career growth strategies
• Increase adaptability in changing environments
• Strengthen professional confidence
• Create a long-term development roadmap

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Students must complete assignments with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will create a Personal Upskilling Roadmap outlining the skills they plan to develop, the learning methods they will use, and how these skills will support their academic, professional, and long-term career goals.

# AI301: Advanced AI Prompting

GENERAL INFORMATION

Course Title & Number: AI301 – Advanced AI Prompting
Term & Year: Fall 2025 – Spring 2026

COURSE DESCRIPTION

Advanced AI Prompting is a hands-on course designed to teach students how to effectively communicate with artificial intelligence tools to increase productivity, creativity, and professional performance. As AI becomes integrated into every industry, the ability to write clear, strategic prompts is an essential career skill.

This course teaches advanced prompting techniques that allow students to generate higher-quality results, automate tasks, solve problems faster, and use AI responsibly in academic and professional settings. Students will learn structured prompting methods, role-based prompting, multi-step prompts, and workflow automation strategies.

Through guided exercises, real-world applications, and interactive assignments, students will practice using AI to complete professional tasks such as writing, research, planning, analysis, and communication.

By the end of the course, students will have a repeatable system for using AI as a powerful career tool rather than a shortcut.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand how AI language models work
• Write clear and effective prompts
• Use multi-step prompting techniques
• Apply AI to research, writing, and planning
• Automate repetitive tasks
• Improve productivity using AI tools
• Use AI ethically and responsibly
• Build prompt frameworks for professional use
• Practice real-world AI workflows
• Create a personal AI productivity system

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Assignments must be completed with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will design and demonstrate a personal AI prompting system that improves productivity, problem-solving, and professional performance.

The SCLA Community:  Compass

We help people develop and elevate their careers by creating opportunities, curating content, and connecting with others.  We break through challenges to find a purpose and unleash the best in ourselves and others.

Our simple framework provides a compass to help us live a driven and purposeful life.

The goal of SCLA Compass is to help individuals maximize their career planning and accelerate their career success. Broadly, that means helping people navigate the next step in their journey—everything from finding a company to join to introducing them to a co-founder, to providing resources they can use to advance in their careers. We hope to build a sort of “momentum engine”—by joining SCLA, you’ll unlock a whole world of information (for example, things like curated lists of under-the-radar companies) that’ll really set you up for success.

So far, things appear to be working quite well—the network already has a few thousand people - people from all sorts of places ranging from new grads to new students on campus all around the USA.   This gives us a lot of energy, and excitement to keep going :).

So what can I expect?

As mentioned, everything we do is designed to support people in maximizing their career success. We try a lot of experiments to make that happen, but here are some of the core pieces you can be on the lookout for:

Curated opportunities - we send a weekly email highlighting new opportunities that include everything from founding teams for internships to scaling startups for mentorship.

Introductions to like-minded peers - we send personalized introductions to people who share your interests.

Invitations to virtual gatherings – we provide lots of gatherings that are unique and distinct and close to you- the perfect way to have engaging discussions in thinking about what’s next in your career journey.

You also gain access to our library, where you can hear directly from world-class founders, investors, and operators who share their own career stories as they dive deep into their journeys and share their learnings.

This sounds great…but I want more!

We hear you. We really do. That’s why we give you access to a whole lot MORE:

More conversations - You can chat with people in our private channels. There’s always a conversation going on — covering everything from freelancing best practices to career advice to just wanting to connect with others.

More discounts - You get thousands of dollars in discounts on our favorite products. Superhuman. Granola. Clay. Replit. And many more.

More opportunities – We also give you access to more direct links to companies and organizations. The platform makes it easy to filter by important attributes like location, role, and other relevant tags.

More connections - The most common ask we get from members is wondering if they can meet other SCLA members. They’re looking for people to brainstorm projects with, get feedback from, and overall just get to know like-minded peers (or mentors, advisors, connectors, etc.). The SCLA Directory makes this easy - you can easily add yourself to it and connect with other members.

More gatherings - Another resource to help you meet others, we also regularly host virtual events that give you an opportunity to connect in different contexts. One event we hosted was with a negotiation expert. Another was just a social to get to know people. Another was around how to pitch yourself in a job interview. Another was about how to come up with ideas. The virtual events tend to be highly interactive, with ample time for Q&A

More content- We get loads of questions from the community — everything from: “how should I choose what company to work at?” to “What questions should I ask my future manager before accepting an offer” — some of which we answer in the form of a more extended form essay. We frequently gather insights from industry experts and share them directly with you on a regular basis.

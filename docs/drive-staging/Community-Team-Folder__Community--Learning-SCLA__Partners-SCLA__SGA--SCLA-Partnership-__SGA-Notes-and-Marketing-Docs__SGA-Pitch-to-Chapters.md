ACCREDITED LEADERSHIP & COLLEGE CREDIT AT NO COST
SCLA is accredited through Array Global, in partnership with the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC),  ensuring our leadership and career programs meet rigorous educational standards.
Why This Matters:
Accredited Curriculum
Recognized leadership certification aligned with high educational standards.
College Credit
Students can earn free college credit, unlike other programs that charge extra.
Institutional Alignment

# Blue True Color Video  

**Slide 1**

Blue:  True Colors

---

**Slide 2**

Blue
Strengths:
Great communicators and listeners
Sensitive to the needs of others
Skilled at building trust and resolving conflicts
Creative and imaginative
Challenges:
May take criticism personally
Can become overwhelmed by others’ emotions
Sometimes avoids confrontation, even when necessary
Examples:
A peer mentor who helps classmates feel included
A counselor or social worker

---

**Slide 3**

Using  BLUE  to Your Advantage
In School:  Lead or participate in peer support groups, student government, or creative projects. Use your empathy to help others succeed.
In Careers : Explore careers in counseling, teaching, healthcare, or the arts.
Personal Growth:  Learn to set healthy boundaries and address conflicts constructively.
Remember:  No color is better or worse than another. Each brings unique strengths to a group or workplace.

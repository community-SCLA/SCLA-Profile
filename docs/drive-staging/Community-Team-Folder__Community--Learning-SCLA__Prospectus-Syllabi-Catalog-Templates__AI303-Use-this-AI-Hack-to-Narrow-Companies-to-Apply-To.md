COURSE DESCRIPTION

Use this AI Hack to Narrow Companies to Apply To is a career strategy course designed to help students use artificial intelligence tools to identify the right companies, roles, and opportunities during the job search process. Many job seekers apply to a large number of positions without a clear focus, which often leads to frustration and low response rates. This course teaches how to use AI to research industries, analyze job descriptions, and find companies that match personal strengths, interests, and career goals.

Students will learn how to use AI-powered tools to gather information, compare opportunities, and make better decisions about where to apply. The course emphasizes working smarter rather than harder by using technology to save time, improve accuracy, and increase the likelihood of getting interviews.

Through guided exercises and applied activities, students will build a targeted job search strategy using AI. By the end of the course, students will have a list of well-matched companies, a clear application plan, and the skills to continue using AI to support their career growth.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand how AI can support job search strategy
• Learn how to research companies efficiently
• Identify industries that match their interests
• Use AI to analyze job descriptions
• Narrow job search targets
• Build a focused application list
• Improve decision-making
• Save time during the job search
• Increase interview opportunities
• Create an AI-supported job search plan

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Students must complete assignments with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will create an AI-Supported Job Search Strategy that includes industry research, target companies, role selection, and a structured application plan.

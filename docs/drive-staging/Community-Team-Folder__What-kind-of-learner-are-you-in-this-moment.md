# What kind of learner are you in this moment

What kind of learner are you in this moment?

Your journey of self development might look a little different than the person next to you, depending on what you need. Take the quiz to learn about areas to focus on, based on your current learner lens.

Explorer

When we explore we love dreaming up big ideas and possibilities that inspire others. These skills and activites help us all decide how to apply our ideas to our lives.

Get inspired and reframe the world around you

You may feel the need to get inspired right now — perhaps you are looking for people that can support you, want to rediscover a sense of optimism or hope, or just need a new perspective on things in your life. Sometimes, a small shift in mindset can help you move forward. You can do this by building up your belief in yourself, looking for inspiration in your day to day, and connecting with the people who are on your team and will cheer you on.

TIPS FOR EXPLORERS

Start by focusing on what makes you feel hopeful and the people in your life who care about you and support you

Try to carve out a few minutes a day to reflect on what’s important to you. See how your values can lead you to discover new interests

Keep track of any insights you have from reflection sessions, either digitally or even in a physical notebook

Celebrate small wins! Just realizing something new about yourself is a victory.

Dreamer

Pause, reflect, and examine your strengths and what matters to you, in order to shape the story you share with the world.

Take your aspirations and turn them into action

You are an expert at imagining exciting possibilities and dreaming up a lot of big ideas for the future. To take your ideas even further, you might need a little push— now is the perfect time to put those visions for the future into action. You can do this by starting to express your ideas in more tangible ways, creating plans to work towards your goals, and learning to adapt and keep going in the face of challenges.

TIPS FOR dreamers

Write a few goals you’d like to accomplish in the next week or two.

Break your goal down into one small item you can accomplish  a day to help you reach your larger vision.

Enlist supporters (friends, mentors) who can help you keep track of your goals

Epicure

When we dream advocates who live by a strong set of personal values. These skills and activities help us apply our values to our lives.

Dive into what matters to you and shape your story

Experimentation comes naturally to you — you tend to explore ideas with an open mind and appetite to try new things. In the midst of all that you do, it can be useful to take a step back and reflect on who you want to be and where you want to grow. You can do this by examining the ingredients that make up who you are, practicing sharing your thoughts and feelings, and becoming intentional about the narratives you choose to tell about yourself.

TIPS FOR Epicures

Examine how you spend your time. What brings you joy and fulfills you?

Develop a plan and track the difference you can make by focusing  in one area

Spend a week just focusing one interest that brings you joy. Share with a friend what you learned about yourself by focusing in one area.

Connector

When we explore we love dreaming up big ideas and possibilities that inspire others. These skills and activites help us all decide how to apply our ideas to our lives.

Use your strengths and interests to make an impact

You have an understanding of who you are right now, and you are craving a way to make a difference. To get started, take time to become more conscious of perspectives that are different than your own and how you can support others. This starts with making an active effort to recognize the experience of people in your life, speaking up about what matters to you, and sharing your strengths back with the world around you.

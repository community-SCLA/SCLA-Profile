# Green True Color Video  

**Slide 1**

Green :  True Colors

---

**Slide 2**

GREEN
Strengths :
Excellent problem-solvers and strategists
Innovative and logical
Values independence and autonomy
Enjoys intellectual challenges
Challenges:
May seem aloof or detached
Can be overly critical or perfectionistic
Sometimes struggles with emotional expression
Examples:
A science fair winner who loves experimenting
A tech enthusiast or programmer
An inventor or researcher always looking for better solutions

---

**Slide 3**

Using   GREEN  to Your Advantage
In School:  Take the lead on research projects or science fairs. Offer your analytical skills to solve challenging problems.
In Careers : Look into careers in science, technology, engineering, or research.
Personal Growth : Practice expressing your feelings and collaborating with others to balance your independence.
Remember:  No color is better or worse than another. Each brings unique strengths to a group or workplace. By understanding your True Colors, you can play to your strengths, work on your challenges, and appreciate the diversity in others—setting yourself up for success in school, work, and life!

COURSE DESCRIPTION

How to Cold Email Your Way to a Job is a practical career development course that teaches students how to create professional opportunities through direct outreach instead of relying only on online job applications. In today’s job market, many positions are filled through networking, referrals, and personal connections rather than public job postings. Learning how to send effective cold emails is a powerful skill that can open doors to interviews, mentorship, and career opportunities.

This course shows students how to research companies, identify the right people to contact, and write professional emails that get responses. Students will learn how to introduce themselves clearly, ask for informational meetings, follow up respectfully, and build relationships over time. The course emphasizes professionalism, confidence, and persistence while avoiding common mistakes that cause messages to be ignored.

Through guided exercises, real examples, and practice assignments, students will write and send professional outreach emails, track responses, and improve their communication skills. The course also teaches how to use cold emailing as part of a larger career strategy.

By the end of the course, students will have a repeatable outreach system and a set of professional email templates they can use to create real career opportunities.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand why networking works better than applying online
• Learn the purpose of cold emailing
• Research companies and contacts
• Write professional outreach emails
• Create strong introductions
• Ask for informational interviews
• Practice follow-up communication
• Build confidence in professional outreach
• Track networking progress
• Develop a personal outreach system

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Assignments must be completed with a score of 80% or higher.

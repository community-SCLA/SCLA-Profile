COURSE DESCRIPTION

The Career Readiness Certification is the foundational course of the SCLA.  In a future increasingly shaped by automation and AI, this course provides students with a foundation for both leadership skill development and learning how to use AI for professional growth.

CRC101 is an interactive, experiential leadership development course designed to guide students through the foundational stages of personal and professional growth. The journey consists of five key steps for completion.

Orientation/Welcome:

Objective: Introduce students to the SCLA, their chapter, and the overall leadership journey.

Includes: The ISPI Strengths Assessment, which helps students understand their leadership styles and communication preferences. This step focuses on building Connections within their chapter and learning about the program's framework.

SMART Goal Setting:

Objective: Equip students to set and track their personal leadership goals.

Includes: An AI-powered tool that helps students define their leadership vision and measure their progress, contributing to their credentials as emerging leaders.

Peer Connections:

Objective: Facilitate meaningful relationships with like-minded peers.

Includes AI technology that matches students based on shared goals, academic interests, and personality traits. By fostering collaboration, this step emphasizes the development of Connections that support personal and professional growth.

Career Connections:

Objective: Provide students with opportunities to build their professional networks and career readiness.

Includes Tools that connect students with professionals for brief career chats, enabling them to gain valuable insights and expand their professional network. This step focuses on helping students advance their careers through professional development.

Certification Completion:

Objective: Recognize and celebrate the student’s leadership journey and achievements.

Includes: A final self-reflection quiz and a personalized AI-generated summary video highlighting their growth. Upon completion of the program, students earn their Credentials and are invited to a certification celebration event (if applicable) or recognized individually.

Throughout the program, students will focus on these COURSE OBJECTIVE & LEARNING OUTCOMES:

Gain knowledge of core leadership principles, effective communication styles, and real-world leadership challenges by engaging in interactive workshops, self-assessment tools, and exposure to accomplished guest speakers.

Build essential skills in self-awareness, goal setting, teamwork, active listening, and problem-solving through hands-on activities, group discussions, and peer accountability meetings.

Develop attitudes of openness, respect, curiosity, and a growth mindset, fostering a collaborative and supportive community of emerging leaders.

Practice behaviors such as reflective journaling, public speaking, goal tracking, and service to others, ensuring the application of leadership concepts beyond the classroom.

The program is structured to be flexible and accessible, requiring approximately 8–12 hours of work over six weeks. Upon successful completion of all five steps, students participate in an induction ceremony, receive a Certificate of Leadership Training, and are officially recognized as members of the leadership community.

These course objectives will be measured through assignments and reflections at the end of each module. These course objectives will be measured through reflection questions at the end of each course module, with a score of 80% needed to fulfill course requirements.

COURSE STRUCTURE & DELIVERY

Adaptive Learning Paths:
The platform utilizes AI to analyze each student’s prior knowledge, learning pace, and level of engagement. Based on this data, the system customizes the sequence, difficulty, and format of modules, ensuring every learner receives content and challenges suited to their needs and strengths.

AI-Enhanced Content Delivery:
Each step of the Career Readiness Certification Program is presented through interactive modules. AI-driven recommendation engines suggest additional resources, such as articles, videos, or practice exercises, tailored to individual learning styles and progress.

Automated Assessments and Real-Time Feedback:
Quizzes, reflections, and skill checks are automatically graded by the system’s AI, providing instant, personalized feedback. This helps students identify strengths and areas for improvement immediately, and the platform adapts future activities accordingly.

Dynamic Peer Collaboration:
Peer Connections are facilitated through virtual rooms and discussion boards. AI tools help match students with peers who have complementary skills or similar goals, optimizing collaboration skills and accountability.

AI-Powered Coaching and Support:
An integrated AI Members Dashboard is available 24/7 to answer questions, clarify instructions, and provide guidance on assignments or technical issues. The system also provides students with reminders and motivational messages tailored to their engagement patterns.

Advanced Analytics and Progress Tracking:
Both students and instructors have access to dashboards that visualize progress, skill mastery, and areas needing attention. AI-driven analytics highlight trends, predict potential challenges and recommend interventions to keep learners on track.

Seamless Certification:
Upon successful completion of all five steps, the system automatically issues digital certificates and badges, which can be shared on professional networks.

This AI-enhanced, online delivery model ensures that every learner experiences a personalized, engaging, and supportive pathway to leadership certification, maximizing both skill development and program completion. While the course is self-paced, there will be designated times when groups can get together to participate in office hours and/or forums with Instructors and other students. Course activities will consist of videos, reading materials, discussion board posts, reflections, and experiential learning projects.

APPROVED ACADEMIC HONESTY STATEMENT

Honesty and integrity are integral components of the academic process. Students are expected to be honest and ethical at all times in their pursuit of academic goals. Plagiarism--to take and pass off as one’s work the work or ideas of another--is a form of academic dishonesty. Any student found in violation of a breach of academic integrity will receive an “F” for that assignment or be removed from the program.

RETAKE POLICY

According to our policy, a student may retake a module, lesson, activity, or course in its entirety if prior attempts have proven insufficient mastery of the learning material (with a score of less than 70%). Unlimited attempts are allowed, provided they occur within the 3-semester time limit to complete the course. Grade points and credits earned in the original attempt will be replaced by those gained in subsequent attempts until a grade of 70% or higher is achieved. The SCLA Learning Team will work individually with students to consider alternatives.

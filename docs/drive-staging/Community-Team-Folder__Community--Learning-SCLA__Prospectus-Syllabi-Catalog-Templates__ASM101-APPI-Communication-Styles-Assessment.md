# ASM101: APPI Communication Styles Assessment

COURSE DESCRIPTION

APPI Communication Styles Assessment is a self-awareness and leadership development course designed to help students understand their communication patterns, behavioral tendencies, and interpersonal strengths through the APPI assessment framework. Effective communication is one of the most important skills for success in professional, academic, and leadership environments.

In this course, students will complete the APPI Communication Styles Assessment and learn how to interpret their results to better understand how they interact with others, respond to stress, make decisions, and build relationships. Students will also learn how to recognize different communication styles in others and adapt their approach to improve collaboration and influence.

Through reflection exercises, group discussions, and real-world applications, students will practice using their communication style knowledge to strengthen teamwork, leadership, conflict resolution, and professional interactions.

By the end of the course, students will have a clear understanding of their personal communication style and a plan for using that knowledge to improve both personal and professional relationships.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Complete the APPI Communication Styles Assessment
• Understand the four primary communication styles
• Identify personal strengths and growth areas
• Recognize communication styles in others
• Improve listening and response skills
• Learn strategies for conflict resolution
• Adapt communication for different situations
• Strengthen teamwork and leadership skills
• Apply communication style knowledge in real scenarios
• Develop a personal communication improvement plan

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Assignments must be completed with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will present their APPI results, explain their communication style, and demonstrate how they will use this knowledge to improve leadership, teamwork, and professional relationships.

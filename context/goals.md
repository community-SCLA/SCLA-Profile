## Goals for scla-profile

### Primary Goal
Give the SCLA Community Team a living knowledge base they can actually use day-to-day — not a one-time snapshot. Specifically:

- **Brand guide** — colors, voice, visual standards; usable in design and comms work without digging through the website
- **Knowledge base** — structured wiki of SCLA's programs, history, team, and how things are named (new team members should be able to read it in under an hour)
- **Workflow map** — current team workflows documented with automation opportunities called out
- **Source of truth** — charter, decisions log, team handbook, and onboarding guide; owned by the team, not auto-overwritten

# Goals — SCLA Community Team

## Mission

Build a robust, peer-driven community where students at any college — including community colleges and trade schools — can find foundational career readiness, professional connections, and a clear path from student to professional.

## Strategic Bets

1. **Focus Modes** — personalized platform experience that lets each member enter at the level they need (resume basics through advanced personal branding). Target launch: August 1, 2026.

3. **Documented member journey** — onboarding SOPs and info session materials live in the Drive and are maintained as institutional knowledge.

4. **Automated weekly rhythm** — Weekly News ships consistently without Sean/Shawn on the critical path for template authoring.

## Goals for scla-profile

### Primary Goal
Produce a living, structured knowledge base for SCLA that:
- Captures the org's brand identity (colors, voice, visual standards)
- Documents how SCLA operates (workflows, cadence, tools)
- Provides a single source of truth for the team (charter, decisions, handbook)
- Makes onboarding new team members fast and consistent

### Secondary Goals
- Establish a reusable template for onboarding future SCLA clients (the pipeline is client-agnostic)
- Surface automation opportunities within SCLA's current workflows
- Keep the knowledge base updatable — not a one-time snapshot but a living document

### What Success Looks Like
- `client/source-of-truth/` contains a complete, accurate charter, decisions log, and team handbook
- `client/brand/` contains an extractable brand guide usable in design and comms work
- `client/knowledge-base/` contains organized articles on SCLA's programs and structure
- The pipeline can be re-run to refresh content as SCLA evolves

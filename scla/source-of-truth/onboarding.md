# SCLA Community Team — First-Week Onboarding Checklist

For new Community Team members. Work through these in order. Ping Kierra in Slack if you get stuck.

- [ ] Get added to **Community Google Drive** (ask Amy or Kierra)
- [ ] Get added to **Slack** and join the community team channels

## Day 4 — Understand workflows

Read:

# Career Readiness Accelerator

Also known as **The Launchpad**. A 5-week self-paced program built on one core idea: the key to accelerating your career is to look inward first. Inner work (values, strengths, purpose) drives outward success (career strategy, networking, opportunity).

## Program tagline

> "Transform Your Career in 5 Weeks"

---

## Structure

Each week (module) delivers:
- **Knowledge** — principles and frameworks
- **Skills** — applied techniques
- **Competencies** — durable behaviors built over time

---

## Module Breakdown

### Launchpad — Get Grounded (Week 1)

Prime your mindset for success. Foundation module: understanding that attitude and inner clarity drive career outcomes.

**Knowledge:** Growth mindset principles, professional communication, networking best practices
**Skills:** Self-reflection, positive thinking, goal-setting, introspection, active listening, strategic planning, creative thinking
**Competencies:** Resilience, adaptability, motivation, self-awareness, emotional intelligence, decision-making, strategic goal alignment

**M1:L1 — Mindset Matters: Shaping a Career-Ready Attitude**
- Reframe self-limiting beliefs
- Cultivate a resilient mindset
- Understand how beliefs (invisible forces) shape habits and potential
- Uncover and challenge beliefs that hold you back

### What is SCLA?

> "The Society for Collegiate Leadership & Achievement (SCLA) is an accredited honor society that celebrates academic excellence and empowers students with the skills, connections, and credentials needed to succeed in college and beyond. With over 100,000 members at 850+ campuses, SCLA is your platform to stand out, grow, and launch your career."

---

### What are the benefits?

> "SCLA membership offers far more than recognition. Benefits include:"

- Career Readiness Certifications from leaders like Google and Microsoft.
- College credit at no additional cost upon completion of specific programs.
- 15-Minute Career Chats with industry professionals.
- Exclusive Job Board Access to top employers and internships.
- Mentorship connections and a nationwide peer network.
- Scholarship opportunities.

### How do I become a member?

Membership is invitation-based. Students are invited when they meet the GPA and credit hour eligibility requirements at a partner campus.

> "Your invitation is your opportunity to join a distinguished network of leaders."

For students at campuses without an SCLA chapter, online membership is available. See [What if my school doesn't have an SCLA chapter?](#what-if-my-school-doesnt-have-an-scla-chapter) below.

---

### What GPA do I need?

From the Eligibility page:

> "To join, you must have accomplished the following:"

- Enrollment as an undergraduate or graduate student at an accredited institution
- Accumulate 12+ undergraduate hours or 9+ graduate hours
- Achieve a minimum GPA of 3.0 (undergraduate) or 3.4 (graduate) on a 4.0 scale
- Maintain good academic standing with your institution

### How do I get my Career Readiness Certification?

From the Program page:

- Equip you with essential career skills valued by employers.
- Award you college credit at no additional cost upon completion.
- Set the foundation for your membership journey, unlocking advanced certifications and exclusive benefits.

> "Every activity you complete earns you Experience Points (XP) that contribute to certifications, badges, and recognition."

---

### How do I earn college credit through SCLA?

From the College Credit page:

> "As a member of the SCLA, you have access to a powerful academic advantage, the opportunity to earn college credit through select SCLA courses at no additional cost beyond your membership."

**Steps:**

1. Look for credit-eligible courses in the SCLA course catalog.
2. Complete all course requirements and assessments.
3. Download your unofficial transcript from your Credentials Wallet.
4. Request an official transcript to be sent to your college or university.

> "These credits are recognized through our accredited academic partnerships and can be a meaningful addition to your official academic record."

### What is SCLA? (Administrator)

> "The Society for Collegiate Leadership & Achievement (SCLA) is a multi-disciplinary honor society with 100,000+ members at 850+ colleges nationwide. ### How does SCLA benefit my institution?

> "SCLA offers a turnkey solution to enhance student engagement and success without additional cost to your institution. Key benefits include:"

- Customizable Programs tailored to your campus needs.
- College Credit and certifications that help students stand out.
- Real-time reporting tools to measure program impact.
- A no-cost partnership model for colleges and universities.

### Is there a cost to host SCLA on our campus?

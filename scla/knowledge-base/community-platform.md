# SCLA Community Platform

SCLA's online member community — referred to internally as **COMMUNITY** — is a structured async-first space for career development, connection, and peer support. The platform operates at **theCOMMUNITY.com** and is built around cohorts, channels, events, and partner perks.

## Community guidelines (key rules)

- Make favor requests clear and actionable — include context, forwardable links, relevant info
- Help maintain safe, authentic, supportive vibes — report behavior that feels off to the COMMUNITY team (handled with discretion)
- Use COMMUNITY primarily as async; IRL gatherings are self-organized by local Chapter leaders

## Onboarding flow (new members)

Phase 1 (Weeks 1–2) priorities:
1. Role rollout — establish community leadership and vision (who leads, why the space exists, what to expect)
2. First impressions — staggered content release: Day 1 welcome, Day 3 first prompt, Day 5 events teaser
3. High-touch presence — replies within 24 hours, model the behavior, tag members to invite participation
4. Platform immersion — community leaders walk through platform as a member to identify friction points
5. Low-barrier early experiences — guided introduction prompts, reflection questions tied to leadership identity

💬 Community Conversations

chat-general
 The catch-all for conversations that don’t fit anywhere else. Pop in and say hi.

 Set goals, check in, and stay accountable with others walking the same path.

chat-ai-new-tech
 Curious about AI or emerging tech? This is the spot to geek out and share how it’s impacting your work or life.

chat-book-club
 Join in on book club picks or just talk about what you’re reading.

chat-reads-listens-watches
 Swap recs for books, podcasts, TV shows, and all your favorite media.

chat-travel
 Talk trips, get advice, and share recs for your next adventure.

chat-health-wellness
 A space for talking about physical and mental well-being, routines, and support.

chat-comp-money-investing
 Discuss comp, salary negotiation, personal finance, and investing.

chat-offline-activities
 What are you doing away from the screen? Share IRL adventures or ask for offline recs.

chat-steps-crew
 Walkers unite! Join others working on movement goals.

 Find local meetups, share tips, and connect with SCLA members in your city or region.

 Identity-based spaces for members in their 20s and 30s to find peers.

 For those supporting loved ones and balancing caregiving with everything else.

 Spaces to connect with others who share your lifestyle or creative path.

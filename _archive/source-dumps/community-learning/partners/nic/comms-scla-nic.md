# Comms SCLA_NIC

[NIC EMAIL NOTICE]

Subject: Important Notice to NIC Fellows

We’re writing to share an important update about the National Innovation Council/Council for Global Health Scholars and to highlight several benefits we are providing to support your continued growth.

After careful consideration, we have decided to wind down our operations at NIC/CGHS due to financial constraints. As part of this transition, we are no longer able to continue providing programs or services through NIC or its platforms.

While this is a difficult decision, we are deeply grateful for your participation in our programs and your commitment to innovation and leadership. In recognition of your engagement and learning with us, we are pleased to offer you:

A transcript reflecting 6 academic credits in Executive Leadership in AI and Innovation, which you may use for your professional and academic portfolio

You will receive a separate email containing your Transcript and verified credentials for posting to LinkedIn or your portfolio.

Access to Innovation and AI content (attached as a PDF)

We want to ensure that you are recognized for your work and engagement in NIC and this Fellowship.  In doing so, we have aligned with the Society for Collegiate Leadership and Achievement (SCLA), a separate organization that will invite you to opt in and join their community as a Society Member.

You will receive a follow-up email from SCLA with an invitation to join and receive a complimentary membership to their program, including ongoing access to resources, networking, and professional development opportunities. The SCLA is not continuing the NIC program; instead, it is inviting you to opt in to its leadership program.  You can learn more on the SCLA website.

This email from the SCLA will provide instructions on how to accept the invitation and opt in to join their community and access all the benefits of their chapters and platform.  Once you join, you will receive immediate Honor Society additional access to their new program, The Academy, as a feedback-driven pilot (launching February 2026).

If you have questions about your transcript, your SCLA membership, or need documentation for your records, please contact us at:

NIC Management Team
Email: support@joinnic.org

Thank you again for being part of our community and for the impact you are making in your academic, professional, and global academic journeys.

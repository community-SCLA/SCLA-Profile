TIMELINE & ACTION STEPS
Phase 1 – Partnership Agreement (April 2025)
Estimated Time: 4 weeks
• Align on goals and roles through initial meetings
• Create and finalize a basic partnership agreement (MOU)
• Ensure legal review and approval
• Announce the partnership publicly
• Begin aligning content, tech, and operations
• Draft a simple plan with responsibilities and timelines
2025 The Society for Collegiate Leadership & Achievement 25

---

TIMELINE & ACTION STEPS
Phase 2 – Implementation & Onboarding (May–August 2025)
Estimated Time: 6–8 weeks
• Train both teams on their roles
• Share tools and resources (e.g., project management, communication)
• Set up regular check-ins to stay aligned
• Launch first projects or initiatives
• Track early progress to confirm things are on track

TIMELINE & ACTION STEPS
Phase 3 – Ongoing Monitoring (Starting August 2025)
Reviewed Quarterly
• Check progress using key performance indicators (KPIs)

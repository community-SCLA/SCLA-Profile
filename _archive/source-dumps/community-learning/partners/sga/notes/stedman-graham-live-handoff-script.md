3. ONE-PAGER: Identity Leadership + SCLA

A Partnership Built on Purpose, Structure, and Student Success

From Stedman Graham

“You can’t lead others until you first lead yourself. That’s what Identity Leadership is all about.

When students understand who they are, they show up with more confidence, more direction, and more ability to create value for themselves, for their communities, and for the world.”

I’ve spent my life teaching students how to define themselves through the lens of identity, rather than letting the world define them. And after decades of speaking and teaching, I’ve learned something critical: students don’t just need motivation, they need infrastructure. They need an environment that helps them put these lessons into action.

That’s why we’ve partnered with the Society for Collegiate Leadership & Achievement (SCLA).

Why This Partnership Matters for Your Institution

Every university wants to develop student leaders. But few programs create the internal shift required for real leadership to begin.

This partnership is built to do just that.

Identity Leadership™ is my 9-step framework that teaches students how to lead themselves first; so they can lead others with clarity, purpose, and confidence.

SCLA is the platform that makes it possible to deliver that transformation on your campus.

How It Works

Your University Establishes a No-Cost SCLA Chapter
This gives students access to SCLA’s nationally recognized leadership and career readiness platform; offering them tools, structure, peer connection, and support.

Identity Leadership is Offered as a Signature Experience Within That Chapter
Through this model, students can enroll in the Identity Leadership course ($180/year) and complete my framework in a structured, supported environment, while earning recognition, mentorship, and professional credentials along the way.

Greg Brisco, Will Guide the Implementation
Once we make the introduction, Greg is your expert partner. He’ll walk you through every step, ensuring your students get the full value of the experience without adding to your administrative workload.

What Your University Gains

A Proven Leadership Framework
Identity Leadership helps students clarify who they are, resulting in stronger academic performance, better decisions, and deeper engagement on campus.

A Fully Supported Program Delivery Platform
SCLA provides the system, the structure, and the people to help you launch and sustain this effort without additional cost to your institution.

A Differentiated Student Experience
This combined program elevates your university’s commitment to student success, career readiness, and inclusive leadership development.

A Partnership Rooted in Integrity and Purpose
We believe in transformation that lasts. This is not just another program. It’s a path to personal power.

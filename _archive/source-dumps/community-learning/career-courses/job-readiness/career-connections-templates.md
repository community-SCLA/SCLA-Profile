# Career Connections Templates 

Asking for Consideration for a Job Role

Prompt + Example Link:  Ask for Consideration

Prompt:

I am reaching out to the [role and name] at [company] via a cold message on LinkedIn.

I don't know the person, but they have a [title] role open. My goal is to introduce myself and explore the opportunity to connect with him through a call sometime in the coming week regarding the open role.

I want you to write this message with the goal of optimizing for the highest possible response rate. To do this:

1. Please review my attached resume. Translate this into a pitch that covers: a) Who am I? - specifically calling out [relevant background], b) What am I looking for: [short version of your criteria/interest in this company]

2. Allude to commonalities - [common connections, domain relevance, alma mater, prior companies, same city]

3. Ask for time with him or someone he recommends from the leadership team to share my background and learn more about the company. I'll be attaching my resume, so please note that.

The message should not come across as desperate. It should stem from a mindset of opportunity and curiosity, yet refer specifically to the open job. It should sound executive. It should be concise.

Avoid using:

1. "-" as a punctuation

2. These words: Transformative, Strategic, Groundbreaking, Commendable, Paramount, Revolutionize

Go!

I am reaching out to the Head of Talent at Incredible Health via a cold message on LinkedIn.

I don't know the person, but Incredible Health has a VP of Product role. My goal is to introduce myself and explore the opportunity to connect with him through a call sometime in the coming week regarding the open role.

I want you to write this message with the goal of optimizing for the highest possible response rate. To do this:

1. Please review my attached resume. Translate this into a pitch that covers: a) Who am I? - specifically calling out my with my background in building marketplaces, especially in complex verticals like chemical industry and construction, b) What am I looking for: work at the intersection of marketplaces, ML/AI, in a complex industry, also mission driven work, which they can offer.

2. Allude to commonalities - 12+ common connections and an interest in building marketplace companies.

3. Ask for time with him or someone he recommends from the leadership team to share my background and learn more about the company. I'll be attaching my resume, so please note that.

The message should not come across as desperate. It should stem from a mindset of opportunity and curiosity, yet be tailored to the specific open job. It should sound executive. It should be concise.

Asking for a Referral & Connection to a HM

Prompt + Example Link:  Asking for a Referral & Connection to a Hiring Manager

Prompt:

I am looking to reach out to a [role and name] at [company] through a cold message on LinkedIn.

I do not know this person, but they have a [job title] position open that I am interested in.

My goal is to introduce myself and request a connection or referral to the hiring manager, ideally leading to a conversation about the open role.

Please write a concise LinkedIn message that:

Introduces who I am, highlighting my relevant background and recent experience

Clearly states my interest in the specific role and company

References any commonalities (such as mutual connections, industry experience, alma mater, or location)

Politely asks for a referral or introduction to the hiring manager, or for a brief conversation to discuss the opportunity

Mentions that I am attaching my resume for context

Maintains a tone of curiosity and professionalism, not desperation

Avoid using the following words: Transformative, Strategic, Groundbreaking, Commendable, Paramount, Revolutionize.

Do not use hyphens as punctuation.

Optimize the message for a high response rate.

—--------

Output SAMPLE:

Hi [Name],

I noticed the [Job Title] opening at [Company] and wanted to connect directly. My experience spans [relevant background or industry], most recently at [Previous Company/Role], where I focused on [briefly describe a relevant achievement or responsibility]. I’m particularly interested in [Company] because of your commitment to [specific value, product, or mission relevant to the company], which aligns closely with my own career goals.

We share [mention commonalities, such as mutual connections, industry background, alma mater, or city], and my background could add value to your team. I’ve attached my resume for context.

Would you be available for a brief call next week, or could you recommend someone from the leadership team I might connect with to learn more about the role and the company culture?

Thank you for considering,
[Your Name]

Networking at Events

Prompt + Example Link:  Elevator Pitch for Networking Events

Prompt:

I am preparing for a networking event, where I will have the opportunity to speak with several C-suite and VP leaders from <describe type of companies> companies that I am targeting for my next role.

Help me prepare an elevator pitch. This one is for the <title and company of the target person>

The goal is to impress the person enough to secure a deeper call, or a call with their hiring team for potential future roles. Since this is high-level leadership, I don't want it to come across as transactional, aimed at a particular role.

It should be in a natural-sounding language containing minimum adjectives and devoid of specific numbers, if at all, you choose to cover impact. It should sound like something someone may say to their friend over a coffee, not overly structured, where I'd come across as a robot.

It still needs to convey:

1. Who am I? (<what couple of things you want to convey>)

2. What am I looking for? (<figuring out my next, waiting for the right opportunity at the intersection of <pick your target>>)

3. Why should they care? (something specific about their company's growth and needs)

Uploading my resume for your consideration. If you need any other information, ask for it.

Let's go!

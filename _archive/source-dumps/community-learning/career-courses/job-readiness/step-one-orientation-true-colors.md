KNOW  THYSELF
The famous quote “know thyself” is often attributed to Socrates and is inscribed in the Temple of Apollow at Delphi in Greece.
It is a call to self-reflection and understanding one’s own strengths, challenges, and motivations.
The  phrase  is also linked to other philosophers like Aristotle who said “Knowing yourself is the beginning of all wisdom.”

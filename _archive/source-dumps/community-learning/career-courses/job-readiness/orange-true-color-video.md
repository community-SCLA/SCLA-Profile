ORANGE
Strengths:
Adventurous and willing to take risks
Resourceful and quick to find solutions
Enjoys competition and thrives under pressure
Excellent in crisis situations
Challenges:
May become bored with routine or detailed work
Can be impulsive or impatient
Sometimes struggles with long-term planning
Examples:
A star athlete who loves the thrill of competition
An entrepreneur who enjoys starting new projects
A firefighter or emergency responder who thrives in fast-paced situations

**Slide 3**

Using  ORANGE  to Your Advantage
In School:  Volunteer for group activities, sports, or projects that require quick thinking and action. Use your energy to motivate others.
I n Careers : Look for roles that offer variety, excitement, and hands-on work—such as sales, emergency services, or entrepreneurship.
Personal Growth:  Practice patience and long-term planning to balance your spontaneity.
Remember:  No color is better or worse than another. Each brings unique strengths to a group or workplace. By understanding your True Colors, you can play to your strengths, work on your challenges, and appreciate the diversity in others—setting yourself up for success in school, work, and life!

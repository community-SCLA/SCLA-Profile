# Career Readiness Accelerator Outline > Syllabus 

The Career Accelerator

WHAT YOU LEARN AND LEAVE WITH:

This program is built on a simple idea: the key to accelerating your career is to look inward first. By understanding your values, strengths, and purpose (the inner work), you can craft a game-changing strategy for your career (the outward success).

Launchpad – Transform Your Career in 5 Weeks

Get grounded, set intentions, and prime your mindset for success.  This module is designed on a simple idea: the key to accelerating your career is to look inward first. By understanding your values, strengths, and purpose (the inner work), you can craft a game-changing strategy for your career (the outward success).

Knowledge: Principles of growth mindset; importance of attitude in career success, professional communication, networking best practices

Skills: Self-reflection, positive thinking, goal-setting, Introspection, active listening, Strategic planning, creative thinking

Competencies: Cultivating resilience, adaptability, and motivation, Self-awareness, emotional intelligence, decision-making, strategic goal alignment

M1:L1: Mindset Matters – Shaping a Career-Ready Attitude

Module 1: Reframe self-limiting beliefs and cultivate a resilient mindset

Beliefs are what you see as true and are the invisible force that controls your habits and potential.

Uncover and challenge beliefs that hold you back. Understand the importance of a resilient mindset in achieving ambitious goals. By the end, you will have the tools to reframe negative beliefs and build a resilient mindset that supports ambitious goals.

Lesson 1: Mindset Matters

Activity: Human Marble Run teamwork game to practice collaboration and patience

Engagement: “Career Mindset” vision board creation in small groups.

M1:L2: Know Thyself – Unlocking Your Passions, Strengths & Values

Lesson 2: Know Thyself

Activity: Strengths & values speed mapping—students rotate stations and jot down strengths for their peers.

Engagement: Self-discovery journaling and quick writes after reflection prompts.

Define your core values to guide your life and shape your character

Values are the flashlight and compass that guide your life. Identify the values that drive your decisions and actions. Learn why aligning with your core values fosters integrity and fulfillment. By the end, you will leave with a short list of values to guide your career choices.

M1:L3: Brand YOU – Crafting a Professional Identity That Stands Out

Clarify your purpose for long-term direction

Purpose is the driving force behind your goals and the guiding star that shapes your direction.

Discover what drives you and align it with your career goals. Learn how a clear purpose fuels focus and commitment. By the end, you will have a purpose statement that provides clarity and long-term focus for your career and life.

Lesson 4: Brand YOU

Activity: “Blindfold Game” paired exercise for practicing communication and branding pitch skills

Engagement: Elevator Pitch Contest—students record and share their pitches for peer feedback.

Assessment 1: Career Mindset Reflection – Write a short essay or journal entry summarizing growth mindset shifts and self-awareness highlights developed during the module.

Assessment 2: Professional Identity Board – Present a visual or digital board showcasing personal brand, core strengths, and professional goals to peers for feedback

Module 2: The Mirror – Get to Know Yourself

Build deep self-awareness to create alignment between who you are and where you want to go.

Knowledge: Career drivers, motivation theories, Goal-setting techniques, visionary planning

Skills: Critical thinking, accurate self-assessment, skill prioritization

Competencies: Identity clarity, informed career choice, intrinsic motivation

M2:L1: Career DNA – What Drives You?

Module 5: Set compelling goals aligned with your purpose

Goals are specific objectives that provide focus, direction, and energy, driving you toward your purpose. Define meaningful goals that align with your vision and purpose. By the end, you will leave with a clarified set of goals to guide your focus and actions.

Lesson 1: Career DNA

Activity: Career Misconception Quiz—what do you think a role is, versus what it really is

Engagement: Group debrief on “a-ha” moments.

M2:L2: The Power/Skill Auction – Your Hidden Superpowers

Lesson 2: The Leadership Skill Auction—students bid on traits using play money to build their “dream leader

Activity: Skill Inventory Worksheet—students trade and discuss which skills matter most.

M2:L3: Aligning Passions and Professions – Making Work Feel Like You

Module 1: Visualize a compelling vision of what you want and why

Vision is a compelling story of what you want and why, serving as a blueprint for your future. You will craft a clear, compelling vision of your ideal future. Understand how a strong vision provides direction and motivation. By the end, you will have a clear, vivid, and actionable vision of what success looks for you to guide your decisions and inspire action.

Lesson 3: Aligning Passions and Professions

Activity: Future Vision Collage—visualizing your dream job using magazines/paper

Engagement: Walk-and-talk partner interviews about passions.

M2:L4: The “Why” Factor – Building Meaning Into Your Path

Lesson 5: The “Why” Factor

Activity: Fishbowl Group Discussion—why are you on this career path?

Engagement: Passion Pitch—students share “why” in one minute or less

Week 5 - Manifest Your Vision

Module 1: Craft a memorable brand to establish authority and influence

A personal brand is the unique combination of skills, experiences, and personality you present to the world, shaping how others perceive and interact with you. Develop a personal brand that amplifies your visibility and credibility. Learn how branding aligns with your vision and career goals. By the end, you will have a personal brand strategy to position yourself as an authority and stand out.

Lesson 1: Career Marketing

Activity: Create a career marketing plan for a “client” (themselves or a peer).

Engagement: Group gallery walk to display “career product pitches.”

Assessment 1: Career DNA & Skills Inventory – Complete a self-assessment on personal values, interests, and preferred skills, supported by a group debrief or reflection paper.

Assessment 2: Future Self Letter – Write a letter from the perspective of your “future self,” explaining your career journey and aspirations to track values and vision alignment

Module 3: The Toolkit – Building For Career Readiness

Develop the practical tools and skills you need to thrive in today’s career landscape.

Knowledge: Networking strategies, informational interviewing, entrepreneurial mindset

Skills: Interpersonal communication, connection mapping, Goal setting, Problem-solving, innovation, cross-industry research

Competencies: Relationship management, professional community building, Growth mindset, Flexibility, creative thinking, Digital agility

M3:L1: Social Capital 101 – Who You Know (and Who Knows You)

Lesson 7: Social Capital 101

Activity: Networking Remix—roleplay introducing yourself in different settings (virtual, in-person, etc.).

Engagement: Social Capital Bingo—students complete networking challenges throughout the module.

M3:L2:  Networking Remix – Making Authentic Connections That Last

Module 3: Build your professional network strategically

A network is a strategic web of relationships that provide support, opportunities, and resources to advance your career. Cultivate relationships that support your career vision. Learn how to expand your network to unlock opportunities strategically. By the end, you will have a networking strategy to unlock new opportunities.

Module 4: Present your career strategy for feedback and support

Self-Awareness is the conscious knowledge of your character, feelings, motives, and desires.
Building self-awareness is a critical step in personal and professional growth. Presenting your insights not only reinforces your understanding but also helps in convincing yourself and driving transformation. In this module, you will compile the insights you’ve discovered and share them with the cohort. This solidifies your commitment to transformation.

Lesson 1: Networking Remix

Activity: No-hands Cup Stacking Challenge—collaborative skills-building game.

Engagement: Networking web: students map their 10 closest connections

M3:L3: Career Marketing – Telling Your Story & Expanding Your Network

Module 1: Create an identity that can deliver on your vision

Identity is the core of who you are, generating patterns of results consistent with how it is structured.

Design a professional identity that reflects your aspirations. Understand the importance of aligning your identity with your goals. By the end, you will have a clear identity visual and statement to reflect who you need to become.

M3:L4: AI Advantage – Smart Tech Hacks for Job Search, Applications & Interviews

Lesson 5: AI Advantage

Activity: AI Tool Smackdown—compare and test different job search apps and AI tools.

Engagement: Mini-hackathon: Who can automate the best resume update?

Assessment 1: Networking Plan – Create and submit a strategic networking map and action plan for connecting with professionals in your area of interest.

Assessment 2: Career Readiness Challenge Portfolio – Compile documentation (photos, screenshots, journals) of hands-on activities completed, including the AI Tool Smackdown and mini hackathon results

Module 4: The Bonus Round – Level Up Beyond the Course

Wrap up with reflection, recognition, and momentum for what comes next.

Knowledge: Reflective practice models, continuous improvement, Career mapping

Skills: Reflection, self-assessment, articulation of growth, digital tool use, strategic visioning

Competencies: Lifelong learning, self-evaluation

M4:L1: Looking Back, Stepping Forward – Guided Career Reflection

Week 2 - Develop Your Competitive Strategy

Competitive strategy is how you compete in the market. Assess your strengths and identify where to compete for maximum results. Learn how to refine your positioning to differentiate yourself. By the end, you will have a competitive strategy tailored to your unique expertise.

Lesson 1: Looking Back, Stepping Forward

Activity: Career Reflection Hot Seat—students share two big takeaways and one career aspiration.

Engagement: Guided Reflection Journals (digital or printed).

M4:L2: Pivot Playbook – Planning for Transitions with Confidence

Lesson 2: Pivot Playbook

Activity: Roleplay transitions between different careers (mock interviews, “day one” scenarios).

Engagement: Create a Pivot Plan infographic and peer review in teams.

M4:L3: Badge of Honor – Certification of Completion

Lesson 2: Badge of Honor

Activity: Certification Ceremony—students design their own digital badges.

Assessment 1: Reflection Journal – Maintain and submit a final reflective journal synthesizing key takeaways, progress, and actionable next steps post-course.

Assessment 2: Digital Credential & Next Steps Map – Earn and share a course completion badge; present a six-month career roadmap to the group using a visual format.

**Slide 16**

A National Leader in Student Success
LinkedIn Networking
Step 1 – Share your profile (if you have a LinkedIn and want to share)
Drop your LinkedIn profile link in the chat in your breakout
Quickly introduce yourself using a 20–30 second “headline” (name, major/role, career desires, key skillset, what you’re interested in)
Step 2 – Quick profile feedback
Each person verbally shares their profile with the group
Focus on one or two things that stand out as a strength for each person
Step 3 – Connect on LinkedIn
Send each other a connection request on LinkedIn
Add a short, professional note like:
“Great meeting you in today’s session – would love to stay connected.”

# Wellness Center Framework DRAFT 

🌿 SCLA Wellness Channel Framework

🌿 SCLA Wellness Channel Framework

Tagline: Reset. Reframe. Move forward.

1. 🎯 Purpose & Positioning

What this channel IS:

A resource hub + peer-supported space (not therapy)

A place to navigate stress, transitions, and uncertainty

A practical toolkit for high-performing students

What this channel is NOT:

Clinical mental health care

Crisis response (redirect to professionals)

👉 This aligns with your note: “focus on offering resources, not therapy”

2. 🧠 Core Themes (Channel Pillars)

Structure the channel around life + career intersections (not just “mental health” broadly):

1. Everyday Wellness

Stress management

Time anxiety (“time moving too fast” insight)

Burnout prevention

Focus + routines

2. Life Transitions

Starting college / graduating

Job searching stress

Identity shifts

“What am I doing with my life?” moments

👉 Directly reflects your discussion on transition as a learning journey

3. Emotional Support Tracks (Sub-Channels)

#grief-support

#job-search-stress

#imposter-syndrome

#burnout-reset

👉 Matches idea of scalable sub-channels for specific needs

3. 🧩 Channel Structure (Plug into SCLA System)

Using SCLA’s Focus Mode architecture , structure the wellness channel like a mini learning journey:

🟢 Explore

“Start Here: Wellness @ SCLA”

Quick self-check: How are you feeling this week?

🔵 Learn

Short guides:

“How to manage anxiety before deadlines”

“How to reset after a bad week”

Partner resources (Headspace, etc.)

🟡 Practice

Weekly prompts:

“Drop your 3 wins from today”

“What’s one thing you’re letting go of this week?”

🔴 Apply

Real-life actions:

Build a 10-min reset routine

Try a “no-scroll morning”

Use a focus playlist during work

4. 🎧 Signature Features (From Your Notes)

🧘‍♀️ Wellness Corner (Pinned Resource Hub)

Meditation techniques

Quick reset exercises (2–5 min)

“In-the-moment” tools (before exam, interview, etc.)

🎵 Low-Fi Reset Playlist

Community-built playlist for:

Studying

Walking

decompressing

👉 Make it interactive:

“Drop your go-to focus song 🎧”

🧰 Resource Navigation Guides

Since you emphasized “teach users how to find resources”:

Create posts like:

“Where to go when you feel overwhelmed”

“Free mental health resources by campus”

“How to use Headspace (SCLA partner) effectively”

5. 👥 Hybrid Support Model (Scalable)

From your notes: support 40,000 students without overwhelming staff

Layered Support System:

Layer 1: Self-Serve

Resource library

Playlists

Guides

Layer 2: Peer Support

Prompt-based discussions

Connection Circles (wellness-themed events)

Layer 3: Guided Support

Trained advisors / champions

Light-touch facilitation (not counseling)

6. 📅 Weekly Programming (Plug into Existing Comms)

Tie directly into your existing cadence :

Weekly Posts:

Monday: “Mindset for the Week”

Wednesday: “Wellness Tool Drop”

Thursday Reset: Tie into Thursday Reboot

Friday: “Win of the Week” reflection

Monthly Events (from Events Framework):

Connection Circles: Wellness Edition

Career Lab: Managing Stress During Job Search

Off-the-Record Talks: Mental health in the workplace

7. 💬 Engagement Loops (Critical for Scale)

Make it participatory, not passive:

Prompts that work:

“What’s been draining your energy lately?”

“What’s one small win from today?”

“What helps you reset after a bad day?”

Micro-actions:

React with emojis (low effort)

1-line responses

Polls (inspired by BIG Welcome style engagement )

8. 🏆 Recognition & Positive Reinforcement

Tie into SCLA’s Rewards & Recognition system :

“Consistency Streak” shoutouts

“Wellness Champion of the Week”

Highlight members building healthy habits

9. 🧭 Navigation UX (Important Insight from Notes)

You raised a key question:
 👉 “Static feature vs Focus Mode?”

Recommendation:

Hybrid approach:

Static presence:

“Wellness Corner” tile on dashboard

Dynamic channel:

Active discussions + events

This ensures:

Always accessible ✅

Still engaging and evolving ✅

📅 WEEK 1: “RESET & AWARENESS”

🟢 Monday — Mindset Reset

Post Title: This week feels fast already… anyone else?

Post:
 Time feels like it’s moving at 2x speed lately.

Before we jump into the week—pause for 30 seconds:

👉 What’s ONE thing that actually matters for you this week?

Drop it below (keep it simple 👇)

🔵 Tuesday — Wellness Tool Drop

Post Title: 2-minute reset (try this between classes)

Post:
 Try this when your brain feels overloaded:

Inhale for 4

Hold for 4

Exhale for 6

Repeat x4

That’s it.

💬 Did it help even a little?

🧘‍♀️ WELLNESS CORNER

Tagline: Tools for when life feels like a lot.

🔹 SECTION 1: QUICK RESET (2–5 MIN)

When you feel: overwhelmed / anxious / stuck

2-min breathing exercise (see Week 1 post)

“Name 5 things you see” grounding technique

Walk + no phone (5–10 min)

🔹 SECTION 2: RIGHT NOW HELP

If you're feeling:

😰 Anxious → Try: breathing + write thoughts down

😵 Overwhelmed → Try: brain dump + pick 1 task

😴 Burnt out → Try: stop early + reset day tomorrow

😔 Low → Try: message someone / post in channel

🔹 SECTION 3: STUDENT REALITIES

Career + Life Stress:

Job search anxiety guide

“What to do when you feel behind”

🔹 SECTION 5: COMMUNITY SUPPORT

🔹 SECTION 6: BUILD YOUR OWN RESET ROUTINE

Simple template:

5-min reset

👉 Night:

1 win from the day

If you're feeling persistently overwhelmed, anxious, or stuck, it’s okay—and encouraged—to seek professional support.

Include:

Crisis resources (region-specific if possible)

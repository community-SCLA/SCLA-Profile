MY CAREER HISTORY
Pull together a bullet-point list of what you've done so far: where you studied, where you've
worked, what skills & qualifications you might have, and any key achievements.
WHERE I AM RIGHT NOW
Write a list of what you're currently up to: current job / position / venture, plus any side
projects, volunteering, etc
WHERE I AM HEADING
Where do you want to be in 12 months? What are your main goals for the 1-5 years? However
vague or concrete, note down whatever comes to you.

How This Works

Part 1: Your Criteria Wish List

Instructions: For each criterion, mark any field where it applies (✓ or ✗). Then rank your most important criteria in the final column (1 = highest priority). Add your own criteria in the blank rows.

Part 2: Scoring Summary

Instructions: For each field, count how many criteria you marked (# checked) and add up the rank numbers you assigned to those criteria (sum of ranks). A lower sum of ranks means that field matched your highest-priority criteria — that's your best fit.

Example: If Health & Medicine matched 5 of your criteria with ranks 1, 2, 3, 5, 7 — the sum is 18. A field matching fewer criteria with higher ranks would score worse.

What This Pattern Tells You

The three fields with the lowest final scores are your strongest project matches. Your advisor will use these, combined with your specific wish list, to select three curated project options from the full catalog.

COURSE DESCRIPTION

Introduction to AI Prompting is a foundational course designed to teach students how to communicate effectively with artificial intelligence systems. As AI tools become common in education, business, and everyday life, the ability to give clear instructions to AI is an important skill.

This course introduces the basics of prompting, including how to write clear requests, provide context, ask better questions, and improve results. Students will learn how different types of prompts produce different outputs and how small changes in wording can make responses more accurate and useful.

The course also teaches responsible use of AI, including understanding limitations, avoiding misuse, and maintaining academic integrity. Students will practice using AI for writing, research, brainstorming, planning, and problem solving.

By the end of the course, students will understand how to use AI tools with confidence and will be able to write effective prompts for academic, professional, and personal tasks.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand what AI prompting is
• Learn how AI responds to prompts
• Write clear and simple prompts
• Use context to improve results
• Ask better questions
• Revise prompts for better output
• Use AI for writing and research
• Recognize AI limitations
• Practice responsible AI use
• Build basic prompting skills

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Assignments must be completed with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

COURSE DESCRIPTION

Navigating Tension: Emotional Intelligence & Conflict Resolution is a leadership and communication course focused on developing the ability to handle disagreement, stress, and interpersonal challenges in a constructive and professional way. In modern workplaces and collaborative environments, the ability to manage emotions, understand others, and resolve conflict effectively is essential for success.

This course introduces the foundations of emotional intelligence, including self-awareness, self-regulation, empathy, and social awareness. Students will learn how emotions influence decisions, communication, and relationships. The course also teaches practical conflict-resolution strategies that help transform tension into productive conversation instead of avoidance or escalation.

Through role-plays, reflections, and real-world scenarios, students will practice listening skills, respectful communication, problem-solving, and negotiation techniques. The course emphasizes personal responsibility, emotional control, and clarity of communication as core leadership abilities.

By the end of the course, students will be able to recognize emotional triggers, respond calmly under pressure, and handle difficult conversations with confidence and professionalism.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand emotional intelligence
• Recognize emotional triggers
• Improve self-awareness
• Practice emotional regulation
• Develop empathy skills
• Communicate clearly under stress
• Learn conflict-resolution strategies
• Handle disagreement professionally
• Build stronger relationships
• Apply emotional intelligence in leadership

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Assignments must be completed with a score of 80% or higher.

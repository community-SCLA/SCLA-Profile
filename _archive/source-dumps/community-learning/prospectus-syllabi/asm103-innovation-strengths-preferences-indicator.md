# ASM103: Innovation Strengths Preferences Indicator™

COURSE DESCRIPTION

Innovation Strengths Preferences Indicator™ is an assessment-based course designed to help students understand their natural strengths, preferences, and tendencies when working on innovation, problem solving, and creative projects. The course introduces the Innovation Strengths Preferences Indicator™ assessment, which helps students identify how they approach ideas, challenges, collaboration, and decision-making.

Students will learn that individuals contribute to innovation in different ways, and that effective teams require a variety of strengths and perspectives. The course explores how personal preferences influence communication, leadership, creativity, and productivity. Students will also learn how to recognize the strengths of others and work more effectively in group environments.

Through guided assessment, reflection activities, and applied exercises, students will develop awareness of their innovation style and how to use it intentionally in academic, professional, and leadership situations.

By the end of the course, students will understand their innovation strengths profile and will create a plan for using their strengths to improve performance, teamwork, and leadership effectiveness.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Complete the Innovation Strengths Preferences Indicator™ assessment
• Understand personal strengths and preferences
• Learn different innovation styles
• Recognize strengths in others
• Improve teamwork skills
• Apply strengths to problem solving
• Develop self-awareness
• Improve communication in groups
• Use strengths intentionally
• Create a personal strengths plan

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Assignments must be completed with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will present an Innovation Strengths Profile explaining their assessment results, strengths, preferred work style, and a plan for using these strengths in academic, professional, and leadership situations.

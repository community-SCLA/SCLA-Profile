COURSE DESCRIPTION

The Top 6 Things You Can Do Immediately to Get a Job is a career readiness course focused on practical, high-impact actions that help students secure employment as quickly as possible. Many job seekers spend time applying to large numbers of positions without a clear strategy, which often leads to frustration and slow results. This course teaches the most effective steps students can take right away to increase their chances of getting interviews and job offers.

Students will learn how hiring decisions are made, how to position themselves as strong candidates, and how to use networking, communication, and targeted applications to stand out. The course emphasizes action, consistency, and smart strategy rather than guessing or relying on luck.

Through exercises, planning activities, and real-world practice, students will develop a job search system they can use immediately. By the end of the course, students will have a clear plan, improved job materials, and specific actions that move them closer to employment.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand how hiring works
• Learn the six high-impact job search actions
• Improve résumé and profile quality
• Use networking effectively
• Practice outreach communication
• Prepare for interviews
• Identify target roles
• Build a job search routine
• Increase interview chances
• Create a job search action plan

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Students must complete assignments with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will present a Personal Job Search Strategy including target roles, résumé improvements, networking plan, outreach scripts, and interview preparation plan.

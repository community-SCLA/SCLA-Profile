# ENT102: How to Launch an App in Four Months

COURSE DESCRIPTION

How to Launch an App in Four Months is a hands-on entrepreneurship and technology course that guides students through the process of designing, planning, and launching a simple application within a structured four-month timeline. The course focuses on practical execution rather than theory, showing students how to move from an idea to a working product using modern tools, collaboration, and clear planning.

Students will learn how to identify problems worth solving, define app features, create simple prototypes, and use no-code or low-code platforms to build functional applications. The course also introduces project planning, teamwork, testing, feedback, and basic product launch strategies.

Emphasis is placed on discipline, time management, and iterative development. Students will work step-by-step through the stages of concept, design, build, test, and launch. No advanced programming experience is required.

By the end of the course, students will have a launch plan and working prototype of an app, along with the skills needed to continue developing future projects.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand the app development process
• Identify real problems to solve
• Define app features and goals
• Learn basic product design
• Use no-code / low-code tools
• Plan a four-month build timeline
• Test and improve ideas
• Work with feedback
• Prepare for launch
• Create a personal product roadmap

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Assignments must be completed with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will present an App Launch Project including concept description, prototype, development timeline, and launch plan demonstrating the full process from idea to product.

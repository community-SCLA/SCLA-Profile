COURSE DESCRIPTION

Time Management: Productivity, Focus, and Sustainable Performance is a personal effectiveness course designed to help students manage their time, attention, and energy in order to perform consistently without burnout. Many students struggle with overload, distractions, procrastination, and difficulty prioritizing tasks. This course teaches practical systems for planning work, maintaining focus, and completing responsibilities efficiently.

Students will learn how habits, environment, and decision-making influence productivity. The course introduces proven time-management methods, focus strategies, and planning tools that can be used in school, work, and everyday life. Emphasis is placed on building routines that are realistic, repeatable, and sustainable rather than relying on short bursts of motivation.

Through applied exercises, reflections, and planning activities, students will develop a personal productivity system that helps them stay organized, meet deadlines, and maintain performance while protecting their mental and physical well-being.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand how time and attention are managed
• Learn productivity and planning systems
• Improve focus and concentration
• Reduce distractions
• Prioritize tasks effectively
• Build consistent routines
• Manage workload without burnout
• Use goal-setting methods
• Increase personal accountability
• Create a sustainable productivity plan

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Students must complete assignments with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will create a Personal Time Management & Productivity Plan describing how they will organize tasks, manage focus, maintain routines, and sustain performance in academic, professional, and personal settings.

# AI101: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

GENERAL INFORMATION

COURSE DESCRIPTION

Introduction to Artificial Intelligence is a foundational course that introduces students to the basic concepts, terminology, and applications of artificial intelligence. AI is rapidly transforming education, business, technology, and everyday life, and understanding how it works is an essential skill for modern learners.

This course explains what artificial intelligence is, how AI systems learn, and how AI tools are used in real-world situations. Students will explore machine learning, automation, data, and decision-making systems in a clear and practical way without requiring advanced technical knowledge. The course also discusses the benefits, risks, and ethical responsibilities involved in using AI.

Students will practice using AI tools, analyze real examples, and learn how humans and AI can work together effectively. The goal of the course is not to turn students into programmers, but to help them become informed, responsible, and confident users of AI technology.

By the end of the course, students will understand how artificial intelligence works, how it affects careers and society, and how to use AI tools in a thoughtful and responsible way.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand what artificial intelligence is
• Learn basic AI terminology
• Explore how AI systems learn
• Recognize common uses of AI
• Understand automation and machine learning
• Identify benefits and risks of AI
• Practice using AI tools
• Learn responsible AI use
• Understand how AI affects careers
• Develop confidence working with AI technology

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Assignments must be completed with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will present a report explaining how artificial intelligence works, how it is used in a chosen field, and how they plan to use AI responsibly in their academic or professional future.

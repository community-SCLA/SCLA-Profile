# CRA201: SCLA Career Readiness Certification

COURSE DESCRIPTION

SCLA Career Readiness Certification is the comprehensive certification program designed to prepare students for success in modern academic, professional, and leadership environments. The certification integrates career development, leadership training, communication skills, productivity systems, and artificial intelligence literacy into a structured learning pathway.

Students complete a sequence of courses, assessments, and applied projects that build self-awareness, discipline, adaptability, and professional confidence. The program emphasizes practical skill development, reflection, and real-world application rather than memorization.

Throughout the certification, students explore career interests, develop professional habits, learn modern workplace skills, practice communication and leadership, and use AI tools to improve performance. The certification also includes multiple self-assessments, planning exercises, and capstone projects that help students clarify goals and take action toward their future.

This course serves as the official certification record and integrates learning across all modules in the Career Readiness Accelerator program.

By the end of the certification, students will demonstrate readiness for academic advancement, employment, internships, or leadership opportunities.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Complete required Career Readiness courses
• Demonstrate professional skills
• Develop career awareness
• Improve communication ability
• Build productivity habits
• Apply leadership concepts
• Use AI tools responsibly
• Complete self-assessments
• Create career and life plans
• Present a final capstone project

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week across the certification period.
Students must complete all required modules with a score of 80% or higher.

COURSE TIMELINE / OUTLINE

Capstone Project:
Students will present a comprehensive Career Readiness Portfolio including assessments, career plan, productivity system, leadership reflections, and a professional development strategy.

PREREQUISITES

Students must complete required SCLA courses included in the Career Readiness Accelerator pathway.

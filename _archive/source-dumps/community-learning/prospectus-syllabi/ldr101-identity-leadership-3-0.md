COURSE DESCRIPTION

Identity Leadership 3.0 is an advanced leadership development course focused on understanding how personal identity influences leadership style, decision-making, communication, and professional growth. Effective leadership begins with self-awareness, and this course helps students explore who they are, how they are perceived, and how they can lead with clarity, confidence, and integrity.

Students will examine the connection between identity, values, beliefs, and behavior, and how these elements shape leadership presence. The course introduces the concept of identity-based leadership, where individuals learn to align their actions with their strengths, purpose, and long-term goals. Students will also explore how identity evolves over time and how leaders must adapt while staying grounded in their core principles.

Through self-assessments, reflection exercises, leadership scenarios, and personal planning activities, students will develop a deeper understanding of their leadership identity and how to use it effectively in academic, professional, and team environments.

By the end of the course, students will create a clear leadership identity framework that guides their decisions, relationships, and future career direction.

COURSE OBJECTIVES

Students will:

• Understand the concept of identity in leadership
• Explore personal values and beliefs
• Recognize strengths and growth areas
• Develop self-awareness
• Understand leadership presence
• Learn how identity affects decisions
• Align actions with purpose
• Improve confidence and authenticity
• Build a personal leadership framework
• Create a long-term leadership development plan

Course work requires approximately 4–6 hours per week for 12 weeks.
Assignments must be completed with a score of 80% or higher.
